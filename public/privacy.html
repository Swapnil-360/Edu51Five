Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Edu51Five is an academic portal for BUBT students. We collect:

  • Email address (when you sign in with Google)
  • Google Drive file metadata (when you upload files as an admin)
  • Usage data for improving the platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide access to course materials and resources
  • Enable file uploads to Google Drive (admin only)
  • Send notifications about course updates and exams
  • Improve our services

3. Google Drive Integration

When you sign in with Google as an admin, we request permission to:

  • Upload files to your Google Drive
  • Create folders for organizing course materials
  • Make uploaded files publicly accessible for students

We only access files that you explicitly upload through our platform.

4. Data Storage

  • Course data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL)
  • Files are stored in Google Drive
  • User preferences are stored locally in your browser

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only used within the Edu51Five platform for educational purposes.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Row-level security in our database

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke Google Drive permissions at any time
  • Opt out of notifications

9. Changes to This Policy
